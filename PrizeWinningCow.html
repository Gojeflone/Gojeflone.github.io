Mission Statement

WORK IN PROGRESS

We consider Gwent, created by CD Projekt RED, to be an extraordinary game. First, CD Project RED shared with us Andrzej Sapkowski's Witcher and gave us the opportunity to explore this magical world firsthand. Now, they have given us what we spent so many hours doing in Witcher 3: Playing the game within the game.

Our goal at PrizeWinning Cow is to further permit you to immerse yourself in this world, both in card knowledge and in lore. So that we may share with each other what we so thoroughly enjoy. Sit down, kick your feet up, and play your best deck. Because we sure will.

Things about Gwentify We Want to Have

  • Deck Builder
    • Avid players of the game will want a place to test out certain builds and compare theirs to others. The Builder will provide a good environment for players to create and share.
  • Deck Rating System
  • Card Database
  • Log In System
    • Users need a way to feel part of the site. We want to communicate a sense of "home".
